History of The School

Brainkids International School was founded in 2005 with a vision to provide a nurturing and stimulating learning environment for young minds. Dr. Amelia Jones, the founding principal, instilled a strong emphasis on creativity and critical thinking. Under her leadership, the school quickly gained a reputation for its innovative teaching methods and focus on individual student needs. In 2012, Mr. David Lee assumed the role of principal, bringing with him a wealth of experience in international education. During his tenure, the school expanded its curriculum to include a wider range of extracurricular activities, including robotics, coding, and environmental science. Mr. Lee also oversaw the construction of a new state-of-the-art science laboratory, further enhancing the school's academic offerings. From 2018 to 2023, Ms. Sarah Thompson led the school with a focus on social-emotional learning and character development. Ms. Thompson introduced mindfulness programs, implemented a restorative justice system, and fostered a strong sense of community within the school. Brainkids International School has consistently achieved remarkable academic success. Students have consistently excelled in regional and national competitions, with many graduates securing admission to prestigious universities worldwide. The school's sports teams have also achieved significant victories, demonstrating the well-rounded development of its students. Beyond academics, Brainkids International School has actively engaged with the local community. The school has organized numerous charity events, supported local initiatives, and fostered strong relationships with parents and the wider community. Today, Brainkids International School continues to thrive, guided by its founding principles of academic excellence, holistic development, and a commitment to nurturing the unique talents of each student. With a focus on innovation and a dedication to providing a world-class education, the school remains a beacon of educational excellence in the region.
